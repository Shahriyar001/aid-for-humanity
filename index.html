Our donate plan

We have three types of flood relief plans.
the details are given below.

Food for a family

We try to ensure that food is delivered to every family in the flood-hit area on time. In the food packets we put some dry food, some fortifying food and pure water. We also pay special attention to nutritious food

Shelter for a family

When the flood situation is under control, we will look at rehabilitation. But now, where possible, human shelters are being arranged. As soon as we find some high and dry places, we start shelter work.

Education for a child

In this situation, we are keeping an eye on the education of the children so that it does not stop. The importance of education in raising the head of a nation can be seen by looking at the present world
